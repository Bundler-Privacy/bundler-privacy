# Privacy Policy  
**Bundler – Digital Membership Wallet**  
_Last Updated: 28 November 2025_

---

## 1. Introduction  
Welcome to **Bundler**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Bundler mobile application (“the App”).

> **Bundler does not collect, share, or sell any personal data.**  
> All card data and scanned information are securely stored **only on your device**.

---

## 2. Information We Collect  

Bundler does **not** require account registration and does **not** collect data on our servers.

The App may store the following **locally on your device only**:

| Data Type | Stored? | Stored Where |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Store name | ✔ | Local device only |
| Membership ID | ✔ | Local device only |
| Member name | ✔ | Local device only |
| Barcode / QR code | ✔ | Local device only |
| Expiry date | ✔ | Local device only |
| Face ID / Touch ID usage | ✔ | Local device only |
| Device analytics | ✘ | Not collected |
| Location data | ✘ | Not collected |

All data remains **offline** and never leaves your device unless exported manually by you.

---

## 3. Use of Device Permissions  

| Permission | Purpose | Required? |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| Camera | To scan membership cards | Yes |
| Face ID / Touch ID | To lock and protect the App | Optional |
| Network Access | Not used or required | No |

We do **not** request or use:
- Background tracking  
- Analytics tools  
- Third-party advertising SDKs  
- Location tracking  
- Contacts or photo library access  

---

## 4. Data Sharing  

Bundler **does not share or transmit data** with:
- Third parties  
- Advertisers  
- Analytics providers  
- External servers  
- Other applications  

We have **no remote servers** and no access to your cards at any time.

---

## 5. Security  

Bundler uses:
- **Apple’s LocalAuthentication API (Face ID / Touch ID)**  
- **Secure local storage using Core Data**  
- **Secure Enclave (where available)**  

If the app is uninstalled, all data is permanently deleted from the device.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy  

Bundler is not intended for children under 13 years old.  
We do not knowingly collect or store information from minors.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy  

If Bundler adds cloud features or online services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.  
Users will be notified through the App or App Store listing.
